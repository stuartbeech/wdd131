Site Plan for "Lego Build Zone"

Site Name

Lego Build Zone

The name "Lego Build Zone" reflects a creative space for Lego enthusiasts to explore, share, and showcase their building projects. It emphasizes fun, creativity, and community around Lego construction.

Optional domain availability: lego-build-zone.org

Site Purpose

Lego Build Zone is a platform for Lego enthusiasts to share creations, find inspiration, and connect with fellow builders.

Scenarios

  • How can I find building instructions for advanced Lego sets? Users can look for detailed guides and resources for complex Lego builds.
  • Where can I connect and collaborate with other Lego builders? This helps users find a community to share ideas, ask questions, and collaborate on Lego builds.

Color Schema

The following color scheme will be used throughout the website:

Primary Color: #F9D20D

Secondary Color: #E74C3C

Typography

The following fonts will be used for the site:

Heading Font: "Bebas Neue", sans-serif

Body Font: "Roboto", sans-serif
